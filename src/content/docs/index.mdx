## Why build on 2D?

2D is a specialized Layer-1 blockchain designed to eliminate friction for both users and developers. By unifying the Ethereum and Tron ecosystems under a single state, it offers an unprecedented seamless experience.

- **One Account, Two Ecosystems:** A single underlying account state natively supports both **Ethereum JSON-RPC** (MetaMask, viem) and the **Tron HTTP API** (TronLink). A transfer sent from TronLink is instantly visible in MetaMask.
- **Zero Gas Fees:** Every transaction is completely free. Instead of charging users, 2D uses an elegant sliding-window throttle to prevent spam without rejecting legitimate high-frequency senders.
- **Instant Finality:** Blocks are final the moment they are created. No waiting for confirmations, no complex mempool management, and zero risk of chain reorganizations.
- **Verifiable Security:** Trust is minimized. Independent read-only verifier nodes cryptographically replay and validate every block before serving state to wallets, refusing to serve any invalid producer state.
